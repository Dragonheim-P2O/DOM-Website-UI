Chapter 1:

FIght to Become the Dragons Leader:

Dragonheim is a fantasy game that revolves around a tragic story of the dragons' survival and the lust of a greedy king who desires to accumulate the treasure of AURA Gold Coins, which is guarded by the dragons for centuries on the island of Dragonheim.

After the king and his highly equipped army attack Dragonheim, the dragons incurred heavy losses. Those that remained had no choice but to escape with the eggs and start a new life of survival in the lost lands of Midgard enduring the most challenging conditions to save their species.

The eggs that were saved during the escape from the Battle of Dragonheim were hatched. Each of them in a different climate where they adapted to the outside environment. When they reached a certain age, the legendary dragons decided to train the young dragons with their wisdom and knowledge. The new dragons have to fight each other to showcase their capabilities and reach the top to become the new leader of the dragon's army in order to claim back their long lost home of Dragonheim and the treasures within it.

Can you become the next leader of Dragons?
